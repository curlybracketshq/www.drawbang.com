Draw! is a web based pixel art editor.
It lets you draw little 16x16 pixel drawings and GIF animations using only 16 colors of the EGA palette.

Follow updates @drawbang and read the blog at http://blog.drawbang.com.

Draw! web app is released under the MIT license and you can fork it at http://github.com/potomak/drawbang.
